Revolut revolutionizes paid plans: access to lifestyle subscriptions

01/11/2023, 7:08:31 AM

Revolut renews its Premium and Metal plans with new partner subscriptions.

By Massimo Costante

Revolut, the global financial app with over 35 million customers worldwide and 1.5 million in Italy, has announced a revolution in its paid plans in the UK and the European Economic Area. The news concerns the Premium and Metal plans , which now include access to subscriptions for the main lifestyle apps at no additional cost. These are a variety of lifestyle subscriptions and beyond ( Deliveroo , Financial Times , Freeletics , Headspace , Picsart , Sleep Cycle , Tinder and more), included in their plan. These additional benefits for the Premium and Metal plans are worth 650 Euros and 1750 Euros per year respectively.

This move aims to consolidate Revolut's position as the go-to global financial app, offering a selection of subscriptions that meet personal and professional needs. The variety ranges from news to fitness, from mental health to dating, offering Premium and Metal users the ability to access premium services easily and immediately, directly from the app.

Tara Massoudi , General Manager Premium Products at Revolut, commented on the importance of listening to customers and constantly innovating products to exceed their expectations. The unique offering announced is designed to bring together a carefully selected package of lifestyle subscription services, offering users more value and convenience in a single account. Massoudi also anticipated the arrival of additional benefits for Ultra customers in the near future.
